### Sign Up: Create Your Account

1. **Go to the EliteFit.AI Website or App**  
   Open the app or visit [EliteFit.AI](https://elitefitforyou.com)


2. **Click on “Sign In”**  
   You’ll find this option on the home screen in the top right corner.

![Sign In 1](/img/SignIn1.webp)

3. **Click on “Sign Up”**  
   You’ll find this option on the home screen below the text fields for username and password.

![Sign In 2](/img/SignIn3.webp)

4. **Fill Out Your Information**
   - Email Address  
   - Password  

![Sign In 11](/img/SignIn11.webp)
