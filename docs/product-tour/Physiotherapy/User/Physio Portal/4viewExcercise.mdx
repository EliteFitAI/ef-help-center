## Patient dashboard

Follow these steps to view your exercise assignments:

1. **Current day's progress**: Ensure the header reads "Today's Session" to view the progress for the current day.

2. **Previous day's progress**: Click on the left arrow icon to navigate to the previous day's assignments and review progress.

3. **Future assigned exercises**: Click on the right arrow icon to explore the assignments for upcoming days.
