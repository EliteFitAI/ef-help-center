## Create Treatment Plan Page

<div style={{ display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '2rem', flexWrap: 'wrap' }}>

<div style={{ flex: 1, minWidth: '250px' }}>

After clicking on the **Create Treatment Plan** icon, follow the steps below to complete and assign the treatment plan:

1. **Choose the user** to assign the treatment plan to from the dropdown list.  
2. **Enter the name** of the treatment plan (example: Posture Correction Plan).  
3. **Select the start date** for the treatment plan.  
4. **Select the end date** for the treatment plan.  
5. **Choose the treatment plan type** (Daily or Weekly).  
6. **Search and add exercises**, then set the number of sets and duration for each.  
7. Click the **"Assign"** button to finalize and assign the treatment plan. 

</div>
