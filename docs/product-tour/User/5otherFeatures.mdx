## Edit Profile

After signing up, it is best practice to edit your profile to ensure your information is accurate and personalized.

<div style={{ display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '1rem', flexWrap: 'wrap' }}>

<div style={{ flex: 1, minWidth: '300px' }}>

![Analytics page](/img/Other4.webp)

</div>
<div style={{ width: "350px" }}>

**Steps**

1. Click on the user dropdown at the top-right of the dashboard
2. Select **"Edit Profile"**
3. Make the necessary changes to your:
   - Name
   - Password (optional)
   - Year of birth, height, weight, weekly workout goals
   - Assigned sex
   - Privacy preferences
4. Click **“Save Changes”** to update your profile

## Support Ticket

To contact our support team for assistance, follow these steps:


<div style={{ display: 'flex', alignItems: 'center', gap: '1rem', flexWrap: 'wrap' }}>

<div style={{ width: "350px"  }}>
**Steps:**

1. Click on the user dropdown menu at the top-right of the dashboard
2. Select **“Support Ticket”**
3. A form titled **“Raise an IT ticket”** will appear
4. Fill in the required information:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone Number (include country and area code)
   - Type of User
   - Description of the issue or query
5. Submit the form.  
   Our team will respond within **24 hours**.
