# Assessments

To begin your movement assessment, please follow the steps carefully. This will help both our physiotherapist and AI system evaluate your movement effectively.

## Why Complete This Assessment?

Before starting, it's helpful to understand the value of this process:

- **Injury Prevention**: Identify potential risk areas before they become problems
- **Expert Review**: Our physiotherapists analyze your movements for optimal guidance
