# Pain & mobility assessments

We’re proud to announce the **official launch of version 1.0** of EliteFit.AI’s AI-Powered pain & mobility assessments – designed to transform how clinicians and patients assess, track, and act on movement health.

## Supported MSK protocols

* **Lower back pain** – clinically validated assessment workflows.
* **Knee pain** – supported with movement AI and patient-reported outcomes.
* **Shoulder pain** – end-to-end guided protocols.
* **Neck pain** – available in *pilot* phase.

## Key features

 **AI-Powered movement analysis**

* Accurate tracking of range of motion (RoM).
* Real-time rep counts and detection of compensatory movement patterns.

 **Integrated patient questionnaires**

* Captures subjective pain levels and functional health insights.
* Delivers personalized recommendations aligned with clinical best practices.

 **Normative benchmarking**

* Performance compared against scientific reference datasets.
* Clear context for both clinicians and patients.

 **Dual-format reports**

* **Clinicians**: In-depth technical breakdown, progression tracking, and treatment guidance.
* **Patients**: Simplified, easy-to-understand summary with clear next steps.

 **Seamless accessibility**

* Available on web, mobile, and downloadable PDF reports.

## How to access

For patients

1. Log in to the [**EliteFit.AI User Portal**](https://elitefitforyou.com/sign-up).
2. Select Assessment from the sidebar.
3. Or visit: [**Assessment portal**](https://assessments.elitefitforyou.com).

For clinicians & admins

1. Log in to the admin portal: [**Admin portal**](https://admin.elitefitforyou.com).
2. Navigate to patient assessments to view results and reports.

✨ **Version 1.0** sets the foundation for the future of evidence-based, AI-driven pain and mobility care. Together, we’re redefining rehabilitation and prevention for clinicians and patients alike.
